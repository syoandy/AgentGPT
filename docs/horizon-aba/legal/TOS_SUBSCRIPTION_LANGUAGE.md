## CLAUSE 1 — Subscription Plans and Billing

```
1. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS AND BILLING

1.1 Subscription. Horizon ABA Health offers subscription plans on a monthly
or annual basis as displayed at https://[YOUR_DOMAIN]/pricing (the "Service").
By purchasing a subscription, you agree to pay the recurring subscription fee
displayed at the time of purchase.

1.2 Automatic Renewal. YOUR SUBSCRIPTION WILL AUTOMATICALLY RENEW at the end of
each billing period (monthly or annually, depending on the plan you selected)
unless you cancel it before the renewal date. By subscribing, you authorize
Horizon ABA Health to charge your payment method on file at the start of each
new billing period without further notice.

1.3 Billing Amount. The renewal charge will be the same amount disclosed at the
time of your initial purchase, unless we have provided you with at least 30 days'
advance notice of a price change at the email address associated with your account.

1.4 Free Trial Conversion. If your subscription includes a free trial, your
payment method will be charged the full subscription amount on the trial end date
unless you cancel before that date. We will send you email reminders 7 days, 3 days,
and 1 day before your trial ends.

1.5 Payment. You agree to provide a valid payment method and authorize us to
charge that payment method for all subscription fees, including renewal fees.
If a payment fails, we may retry the charge or suspend your account.
```

## CLAUSE 2 — Cancellation

```
2. CANCELLATION

2.1 How to Cancel. You may cancel your subscription at any time by:
(a) Logging into your account and navigating to Account → Subscription → Cancel; or
(b) Emailing support@[YOUR_DOMAIN] with "Cancellation Request" in the subject line.

2.2 Cancellation Confirmation. We will send you a written confirmation of your
cancellation by email within 24 hours.

2.3 Access After Cancellation. After cancellation, you will continue to have
access to the Service until the end of your current billing period. You will
not be charged again after cancellation.

2.4 No Refunds for Partial Periods. Subscription fees are not refundable on a
prorated basis for partial billing periods, except where required by law.

2.5 California Subscribers. If you reside in California, you may cancel your
subscription online at any time without contacting customer support, in accordance
with California Business and Professions Code §17602. The cancellation link is
available at [CANCEL_URL].

2.6 New York Subscribers. If you reside in New York, you may cancel your
subscription online at any time, in accordance with New York General Business
Law §527-a.
```

## CLAUSE 3 — Refunds

```
3. REFUNDS

3.1 General Policy. Subscription fees are non-refundable except as required by law
or as specifically provided in this section.

3.2 Trial Period. If you cancel during your free trial period, you will not be charged.

3.3 First 14 Days. If you cancel within 14 days of your initial paid subscription
(after the free trial, if any), you may request a full refund by emailing
billing@[YOUR_DOMAIN]. Refunds will be processed within 10 business days to your
original payment method.

3.4 Disputes. If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact us at billing@[YOUR_DOMAIN]
before initiating a chargeback. We will work to resolve billing disputes promptly.
```

## CLAUSE 4 — Price Changes

```
4. PRICE CHANGES

We may change subscription prices at any time, but any price increase will not
apply to your current subscription until renewal. We will notify you of price
changes at least 30 days before they take effect by email to the address
associated with your account. If you do not want to renew at the new price,
you may cancel before your next renewal date.
```
