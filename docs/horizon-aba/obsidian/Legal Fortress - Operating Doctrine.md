# Legal Fortress — Operating Doctrine

## What It Is
The **structural legal defense system** of the empire. Counselor is the operational head; FEEP defines strategy; this document defines the components.

## The 5 Pillars
1. **Corporate Veil Protection** — formation, capitalization, no commingling
2. **Contract Architecture** — standard contract library, all reviewed by Counselor
3. **IP Protection** — assignments, trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets
4. **Insurance Stack** — GL, E&O, cyber, D&O, plus Tier-specific
5. **Litigation Readiness** — retention, hold procedures, outside counsel rolodex
