# Founder Succession Doctrine

## Purpose
Defines what happens to empire operations when Founder Yoandy OS is unavailable, incapacitated, or deceased.

## States
- **Available** — default
- **Limited Availability** — emergencies only
- **Unavailable** — no response 7+ days
- **Incapacitated** — medical/legal determination
- **Deceased** — death certificate

## Continuity Authority
Counselor + Chief of Staff jointly hold interim authority during Unavailable / Incapacitated states. Limited to routine ops — no strategic decisions, no acquisitions, no equity, no litigation initiation.

## Acting Authority Designation
Founder pre-designates Acting Authority for personal matters during incapacity. Counselor + Chief of Staff handle empire operations separately.

## Action Required
- [ ] Founder designates Acting Authority (name + contact)
- [ ] Founder executes personal estate documents (will, trust, POAs)
- [ ] Annual succession rehearsal scheduled
