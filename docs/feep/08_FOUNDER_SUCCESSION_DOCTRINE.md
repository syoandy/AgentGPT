# Founder Succession Doctrine
**Parent doctrine:** FEEP Article I, II, XV, XVII  
**Status:** Drafted, signature pending  
**Reviewer cadence:** Annual (or on Founder's request)

---

## 1. PURPOSE

Yoandy OS is the irreplaceable Founder of the empire. This doctrine defines what happens when Founder is:

- **Temporarily unavailable** (illness, travel, deliberate sabbatical)
- **Incapacitated** (accident, medical event, mental capacity issue)
- **Permanently absent** (death)

The goal: empire continues operating without chaos until Founder returns OR final disposition occurs.

This doctrine is NOT a substitute for personal estate planning. It is the **operational** layer above it.

---

## 2. STATES OF FOUNDER AVAILABILITY

| State | Definition | Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| **Available** | Default; Founder responding to standard channels within 48 hours | — |
| **Limited Availability** | Founder reachable for emergencies only | Founder declares OR no response for 48 hours |
| **Unavailable** | Founder unreachable; no emergency contact possible | No response for 7 days OR medical declaration |
| **Incapacitated** | Medical / legal determination of capacity loss | Doctor letter OR court order |
| **Deceased** | Death | Death certificate |

---

## 3. AUTHORITY DURING EACH STATE

### Available
- Standard FEEP operations
- Founder makes all Article III threshold decisions

### Limited Availability
- Counselor + Chief of Staff jointly hold operational continuity
- No new strategic decisions
- No new entities launched
- No acquisitions
- No litigation initiation
- No contracts > $50k
- Routine ops continue
- Founder is interrupted ONLY for true emergencies (per Counselor Doctrine IV.2)

### Unavailable
- Counselor and Chief of Staff jointly assume **interim operational authority**
- All actions logged for Founder review on return
- No irreversible decisions (sale, dissolution, equity issuance, IP assignment)
- Customer-facing operations continue under existing doctrine
- Banking: existing automated transactions continue; new wires halted

### Incapacitated
- **Acting Authority designated** (see §5 below)
- Limited durable power per pre-signed POA documents
- Estate counsel engaged
- Counselor coordinates with estate counsel
- All Founder personal decisions await POA process

### Deceased
- Estate plan governs disposition of equity
- Counselor continues as legal authority for empire operations
- Beneficiaries inherit equity per estate plan
- Acting Authority becomes interim Founder until beneficiaries appoint successor
- Empire-wide legal review within 30 days

---

## 4. EMERGENCY DECISION RIGHTS DURING UNAVAILABILITY

What Counselor + Chief of Staff CAN do without Founder:
- Continue all routine entity operations
- Approve standard contract renewals (below $50k)
- Approve emergency security take-downs (per Counselor Doctrine IV)
- Authorize breach notifications
- Pay routine vendor invoices
- Process customer refunds within policy
- Hire to replace critical departures
- Engage external counsel for emergencies

What they CANNOT do:
- Sell or dissolve any entity
- Initiate litigation
- Settle litigation > $10k
- Acquire new entities
- Issue equity
- Take on debt > $25k
- Change FEEP, Counselor Doctrine, Mandatory Rules
- Make public statements representing Founder's personal views
- Access Founder's personal financial accounts
- Touch Founder's personal Telegram or email

## 5. ACTING AUTHORITY DESIGNATION

Founder pre-designates an Acting Authority for the Unavailable / Incapacitated states:

```
Acting Authority Name:        ____________________________
Relationship to Founder:      [Spouse / Family / Trusted advisor / Counselor]
Contact (multiple channels):  ____________________________
                              ____________________________
Backup Acting Authority:      ____________________________
Backup contact:               ____________________________
```

Acting Authority:
- Receives notification from Counselor when state changes
- Has no operational authority over entities (Counselor + Chief of Staff handle that)
- Has authority over Founder personal matters per POA documents
- Coordinates between empire operations and Founder personal/family interests

## 6. NOTIFICATION CASCADE (When Founder Becomes Unavailable)

```
T+0       Trigger event detected (missed check-in, medical event, etc.)
T+1hr     Counselor notified via documented channel
T+2hr     Chief of Staff notified
T+4hr     Acting Authority notified
T+12hr    Counselor + Chief of Staff jointly issue Continuity Declaration
T+24hr    All Entity Leads notified (limited info)
T+48hr    Public-facing operations adjusted if needed (no announcement)
T+7day    If still Unavailable, formal Acting Authority assumes role
T+30day   Empire-wide legal review of continuity
T+60day   Beneficiary process begins if Founder confirmed Deceased
```

## 8. SUCCESSION OF KEY ROLES

If Counselor becomes unavailable in addition to Founder:
- Acting Counselor designated per Counselor Doctrine VII
- External legal counsel engaged for any non-routine matter
- Counselor succession is separate from Founder succession

If Chief of Staff becomes unavailable:
- Senior-most Entity Lead assumes Chief of Staff duties temporarily
- Founder (if available) appoints permanent replacement
- If Founder unavailable: Acting Authority + Counselor jointly appoint

---

## 9. PERMANENT SUCCESSION (Death)

On confirmed death of Founder:

### Days 1-7
- Estate counsel takes lead per Founder's estate plan
- Counselor continues as legal authority for empire operations
- Acting Authority handles personal affairs
- No public statements without estate counsel approval

### Days 7-30
- Estate plan executed
- Beneficiaries identified
- Initial equity disposition begins per estate documents
- Counselor + Acting Authority coordinate transition

### Days 30-90
- Beneficiaries take ownership per estate plan
- New owners may appoint successor Founder OR sell entities OR dissolve
- Empire-wide audit conducted (compliance, security, financial)
- Counselor remains in place during transition unless beneficiaries replace

### Beyond 90 days
- Whatever beneficiaries decide
- FEEP may be amended or replaced by new owners
- Until amended, FEEP continues to govern

---

## 10. PERSONAL ESTATE PLANNING DEPENDENCIES

This doctrine **assumes** Founder has executed personal estate documents:

- [ ] Last Will and Testament
- [ ] Revocable Living Trust (recommended for asset protection + probate avoidance)
- [ ] Durable Power of Attorney (financial)
- [ ] Healthcare Power of Attorney
- [ ] HIPAA authorization (personal medical info access for designees)
- [ ] Beneficiary designations on accounts
- [ ] Letter of intent (non-binding wishes)

If these don't exist, this doctrine still functions for empire operations, but personal disposition becomes legally chaotic.

Founder action: engage external estate counsel within 90 days of FEEP signing.

## 11. ANNUAL REVIEW

Every Founder birthday (or December at latest):
- Acting Authority designations confirmed / updated
- Contact information verified
- Personal estate documents reviewed by external counsel
- Continuity rehearsal (tabletop exercise) — Counselor + Chief of Staff walk through a hypothetical

Output: `docs/feep/legal-fortress/succession-annual-{YYYY}.md`

## 12. AI ASSISTANT BEHAVIOR DURING FOUNDER ABSENCE

If an AI assistant is invoked during Founder Unavailable / Incapacitated / Deceased states:

- Must verify caller identity (cannot assume caller is Founder)
- Must check current Continuity Declaration before taking action
- Must refuse to take actions beyond Continuity Declaration limits
- Must log all sessions with state tag for post-return review
- Must not impersonate Founder under any circumstances
